18th June 2026

Haldi Ceremony

6:00 AM - 8:00 AM

The sacred Haldi ceremony where turmeric paste is applied to the bride and groom in their respective homes. This auspicious ritual is believed to bless the couple with prosperity and ward off evil spirits. The yellow hue symbolizes purity and new beginnings.

Wedding Ceremony (Lagna)

12:00 PM - Until your arrival

The main event! The sacred Maharashtrian wedding ceremony where Tejal and Omkar will exchange vows and garlands under the traditional mandap. Witness the beautiful rituals including the Antarpat, Kanyadaan, and Saptapadi as they begin their journey as husband and wife.
