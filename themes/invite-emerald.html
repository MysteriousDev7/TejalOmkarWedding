We Are Getting Married!

Join us as we celebrate the union of two souls who have journeyed together since childhood. From innocent friends to life partners, our story is one of love, laughter, and endless memories. We invite you to be part of our special day as we begin this beautiful new chapter together.

Our Love Story

A journey that began in childhood and blossomed into forever

1995-2000

Childhood Friends

It all started in the neighborhood playground where two curious kids met. Little did we know that the friend we made while building sandcastles would one day become our life partner. Our innocent friendship was filled with shared tiffins, bicycle rides, and endless giggles.

2000-2010

School Days

Through the years of homework, exams, and school festivals, our bond grew stronger. We were each other's study partners, confidants, and partners in crime. From sharing lunch boxes to celebrating victories together, every moment was special.

2013-2017

College Years

College brought new adventures and deeper conversations. Late-night study sessions turned into heart-to-heart talks. We discovered shared dreams, supported each other's ambitions, and realized that our friendship was evolving into something more profound.

2017-2022

Realizing Our Love

As we stepped into the professional world, distance couldn't diminish what we felt. Weekend meetups, endless phone calls, and constant support made us realize - this wasn't just friendship anymore. We had fallen deeply in love with our best friend.

2025

The Proposal

On a beautiful evening at our favorite spot from childhood, Omkar got down on one knee. With tears of joy and hearts full of love, we decided to make our forever official. The playground where it all began witnessed the beginning of our new journey.

June 19, 2026

Our Big Day

And now, surrounded by family and friends who have watched our love story unfold, we're ready to say "I do". Join us as we celebrate not just a wedding, but a lifetime of friendship, love, and togetherness.

Wedding Events

A celebration of love, tradition, and togetherness

🎨
17th June 2026

Mehendi Ceremony

3:00 PM - 7:00 PM

A vibrant and joyous ceremony where intricate henna designs adorn the bride's hands and feet. This traditional ritual is accompanied by folk songs, dance, and celebration. The rich aroma of mehendi and the sound of laughter fill the air as women gather to bless the bride.

Dress Code: Bright traditional wear - Yellow, Green, or Orange
✨
18th June 2026

Haldi Ceremony

10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

The sacred Haldi ceremony where turmeric paste is applied to the bride and groom in their respective homes. This auspicious ritual is believed to bless the couple with prosperity and ward off evil spirits. The yellow hue symbolizes purity and new beginnings.

Dress Code: Comfortable traditional attire in Yellow
🎵
18th June 2026

Sangeet Night

7:00 PM - 11:00 PM

Get ready for an evening of music, dance, and entertainment! Both families come together to perform choreographed dances, sing songs, and celebrate the upcoming union. It's a night of friendly competition, laughter, and unforgettable memories.

Dress Code: Glamorous Indo-Western or Traditional attire
💍
19th June 2026

Wedding Ceremony (Lagna)

6:00 PM - 10:00 PM

The main event! The sacred Maharashtrian wedding ceremony where Tejal and Omkar will exchange vows and garlands under the traditional mandap. Witness the beautiful rituals including the Antarpat, Kanyadaan, and Saptapadi as they begin their journey as husband and wife.

Dress Code: Traditional Maharashtrian - Nauvari Saree/Paithani for ladies, Kurta-Pyjama/Sherwani for gentlemen
🎉
19th June 2026

Reception

10:00 PM - 1:00 AM

Join us for an elegant reception dinner as we celebrate the newlyweds! Enjoy delicious Maharashtrian cuisine, music, and dancing. It's time to shower the couple with blessings and create beautiful memories together.

Dress Code: Formal/Semi-Formal attire

Dress Code Guide

Embrace the colors and traditions of Maharashtra

For Ladies

  • Nauvari Saree: Traditional 9-yard Maharashtrian saree
  • Paithani Saree: Elegant silk saree with traditional motifs
  • Lehenga Choli: Perfect for Sangeet and Mehendi
  • Anarkali Suits: Graceful and comfortable option
  • Colors: Maroon, Gold, Orange, Green, Pink, Purple

For Gentlemen

  • Kurta-Pyjama: Classic traditional attire
  • Sherwani: Elegant choice for the wedding ceremony
  • Nehru Jacket: Modern twist on traditional wear
  • Dhoti-Kurta: Traditional Maharashtrian style
  • Colors: Cream, Gold, Maroon, Navy, Burgundy
